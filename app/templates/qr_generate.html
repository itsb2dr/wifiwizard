WiFi QR Code Generator

Why do I need a WiFi QR Code generator?

Before this, there was no other way to join a WiFi network without manually selecting one and entering a password. This QR Code lets you join instantly.

My WiFi network is hidden, can I still create a QR Code?

Absolutely. Just tick the “Hidden” checkbox next to the SSID field in our QR generator. Most routers support this feature.

Which security protocol do I need for my WiFi? WPA/WPA2, WEP or None?

WPA/WPA2 is the most secure and widely supported option. Use it unless you're dealing with legacy devices.
